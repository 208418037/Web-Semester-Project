Organization and Functions of th DGPA

  • In accordance with the DGPA Organization Act, the DGPA has one Minister, one Political Deputy Minister, and one Administrative Deputy Minister. It sets up five departments, four offices, and two subordinate institutes. They are in charge of the overall personnel administration of ministries and subordinate agencies under the Executive Yuan. However, the DGPA 's organizational functions concerning civil service examinations and qualifications are overseen by the Examination Yuan.
